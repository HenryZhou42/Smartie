# Company Policy

## Vacation Policy

Smartie Test Corp accrues vacation based on years of continuous service.

| Years of service | Annual vacation days |
|------------------|----------------------|
| First 3 years | **15 vacation days** |
| After 3 years | **20 vacation days** |
| After 10 years | **25 vacation days** |

Employees may carry forward up to 5 unused days into the next calendar year.
